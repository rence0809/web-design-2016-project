NORWAY

This Norwegian capital sits at the head of Oslo Fjord. This stunning setting gives hints of the wild wonders that lie just beyond the city. It is fringed with forests, hills and lakes awash with opportunities for outdoor activities. It is where Mother Nature created one of her finest works of art. During winter, Norway boasts its winter activities, from skiing to sledging name it and they have it! Being there during winter made our experience in Norway to be one of the best of all! It was such a winter wonderland, everywhere you look was a mountain of snow and the best part? It wasn’t as cold as Ireland! I swore I was never leaving the country but I had to get back to reality. I couldn’t afford to stay there any longer as it was much more expensive than euro. But it was all worth it in the end even if we were left broke af. If you’re the adventurous type, I would totally recommend going there especially during winter. If you have the extra time and money, why not pay a visit to the northern part of the country and watch the Aurora Borealis right before your eyes. Norway have so much to offer and the place is so chill you could almost disappear from reality.

Is the largest ski jumping i've ever seen and it is located at Holmenkollen. It is very high ski jumping and there are 2 things that you can go up such as; cable car and stairs. When you reach the top you can see the beautiful city of norway.







Viking Ship Museum

Inside of the museum you will see the well-preserved oldest wooden viking ships that was built in 9th century, you can also see their things such as; tools, textiles and household utensils, small boats, jewellries and many more. The entracne fee is €8 for adults and €5 for students/seniors.

Vigeland Sculpture Park

One of the best and visited park in oslo. Inside of the park you'll find more than 200 naked sculptures in bronze, granite and wrought iron. It was all work of Gustav Vigeland. The famous sculpture is the the angry boy and it was located in the middle of the bridge. Unfortunately, when we went there it was winter time so it's not that so beautiful. but the norwegian told us that it is beautiful during summber because there are more different kinds of flowers will bloom in the park.



Tryvann Ski Resort & Sledge
